## üöÄ Projects

### üîπ Power BI
1. **Product Order Data Analysis**
   - **Dataset**: Order & delivery data
   - **Business Questions**:
     - Which products had high/low orders by state & region?
     - How revenue varied by day, month, year
     - Total orders placed
   - **Insights**: Helps optimize inventory and supply chain.

2. **Social Media Sentiment Analysis**
   - **Dataset**: Social media engagement metrics
   - **Business Questions**:
     - Which platforms drive the most engagement & revenue?
     - Month-on-month performance trends
     - ROI of campaigns
   - **Insights**: Supports marketing ROI and budget allocation.

---

### üîπ Tableau
3. **Sales Dashboard**
   - **Dataset**: Sales transactions
   - **Business Questions**:
     - KPIs: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Customers
     - Product segment & category performance
     - Location & region-wise sales
     - Month-on-month and year-wise trends
   - **Insights**: Enables pricing, product focus, and regional planning.

4. **Customer Analysis Dashboard**
   - **Dataset**: Kaggle Amazon Sales FY 2020-2021
   - **Business Questions**:
     - Regions & states generating maximum revenue
     - Preferred payment methods
     - Impact of cancellations vs deliveries
     - Discount influence on orders
     - Age group ordering patterns
   - **Insights**: Helps understand customer behavior and target promotions.

5. **HR Data Analytics**
   - **Dataset**: Kaggle HR Attrition
   - **Business Questions**:
     - Department-wise employee count
     - Education & gender distribution
     - Attrition rate across HR, Sales, R&D
     - Impact of job satisfaction, income, work-life balance
     - Age group trends in attrition
   - **Insights**: Supports employee retention and workforce planning.
